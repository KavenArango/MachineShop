ABOUT

The machine shop is a resource for students of all majors to take advantage of as an experience both beneficial in a working sense and on a hobbyist level. The machine shop provides experience in hand tools, lathe machining, vertical milling,CNC milling and more. Individual machine bookings are available for all students as well as workshops are booked for students.
